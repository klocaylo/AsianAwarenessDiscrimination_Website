What is the purpose of this website?

This website was created to bring attention to Asian American discrimination and provide information concerning this issue. Asian Americans are facing racial discrimination due to the recent COVID-19 pandemic - they are facing racism because of their race and culture. Individuals within the Asian community have been accused and attacked, both verbally and physically, for supposedly causing this pandemic. This rise in racial discrimination has caused anxiety and discord towards Asians within American society, thus resulting in many Asian Americans fearing to step back into society. With this website we hope to encourage people to start a discussion within their communities discussing not only the seriousness of this issue, but also what they can do to help. These acts of discrimination cannot be ignored, we must stay informed.
